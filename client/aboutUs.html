About Om Sai Trading Co.

We feel pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the leading and reputed suppliers to various govt. and industrial units. Om Sai Trading Co. works on a business-to-business model. We work with multiple reputed brands like Philips, Polycab, Bajaj Electrics and several others to provide best quality of products possible to our customers.

The Beginning

Om Sai Trading Co. started with its humble beginning in 2010. With a small capital and funding we started operations in Chandni Chowk, Delhi. With dedication and hardwork of all the members involved, Om Sai Trading Co. grew and found the firm needed to be handled properly. In 2019, another branch of our company was opened.

Om Sai Trading Co. Today

Today Om Sai Trading Co. is one of the largest suppliers, supplying multiple products (mainly electrical components) to all types of large organizations: government, private and public.
